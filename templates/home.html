This is a simple app you can use to collect and visualize data about how comfortable people are outside! This can be used to catalog hot and cold spots in a city!

To get started, click on the "Form" link in the navigation bar above. There will be two questions about:
1) How much clothing you're wearing.
2) How cold you're feeling.
Once you submit the form, you can click on the "Map" link to see your data visualized on a map along with all other inputs!
